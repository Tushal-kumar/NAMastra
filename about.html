About Namastra

A name is the first gift a parent gives — we help you understand it fully

Our Mission

Namastra was born from a simple belief: every name is a story waiting to be told.

Names are not arbitrary labels. They carry the weight of centuries — shaped by religion, geography, philosophy, and love. A mother naming her child after a Sanskrit goddess, a father choosing a Quranic virtue, grandparents passing down a Hebrew legacy — in each act, an entire civilization is being passed forward.

Namastra uses advanced AI trained on etymological databases, religious texts, and cultural archives to give you the richest, most accurate picture of any name — in seconds, for free.
